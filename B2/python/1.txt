Now that I am a college student, I no longer have to wear a uniform, and the dress code is much more flexible. However, it reminds me of the good old days in high school, when uniforms were compulsory. Boys usually wore a white polo shirt with trousers, while girls wore ao dai, our traditional costume, every Monday. On other days, girls dressed the same as boys. Personally, I sometimes found the uniform a bit uncomfortable, but it created a sense of equality and discipline among students, which I really appreciate now.
